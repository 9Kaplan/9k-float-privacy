9K Float Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Local-first behavior

9K Float stores saved area metadata locally on your device. Metadata can include the area name, source URL/domain, source tab id when available, selected rectangle coordinates, permission/readiness state, and local UI settings.

9K Float may also store a small compressed local preview thumbnail for each saved area. Preview thumbnails may include visible text or form content in areas you choose to save. Preview thumbnails are never uploaded, synced, analyzed, sold, or shared.

What 9K Float does not do

  • No analytics, telemetry, tracking, ads, cloud sync, or remote APIs.
  • No video recordings.
  • No structured form field values.
  • No remote code.

Picture-in-Picture and tab capture

When you open a saved floating area, Chrome captures the visible source tab locally so 9K Float can crop the selected rectangle into a local video Picture-in-Picture stream. The source tab must stay open for live floating to work. Chrome can show only one Picture-in-Picture window at a time. Some pages may be unsupported because of browser restrictions.

Chrome Web Store Limited Use

The use of information received from Chrome extension APIs will adhere to the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
