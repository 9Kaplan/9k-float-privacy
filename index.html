Local-first floating areas for Chrome
Privacy Support

Keep a selected part of a webpage visible while you work.

9K Float lets you select a visible area of a supported webpage, save it locally, and open it in Chrome Picture-in-Picture while the source tab stays open.

Select and float

Choose a rectangular area from a page and keep that area visible in a floating Picture-in-Picture window while browsing other tabs.

Manage saved areas

Save areas locally, reopen them from the library, rename them, delete them, and adjust their position or zoom.

Local-first by design

Saved area metadata and small local preview thumbnails stay on your device. There is no cloud sync, analytics, telemetry, tracking, remote API, or ads.

Important browser limitations

  • Chrome can show only one Picture-in-Picture window at a time.
  • The source tab must stay open for the floating area to keep updating.
  • Some protected pages, browser pages, and restricted sites may not be supported.
